Realtime Prompt Token Counter

Tells you before you run your agent whether your prompt will be rejected at session.update against the 16,384-token instructions ceiling. Counts with o200k_base, then applies a gpt-realtime correction for non-Latin scripts (calibrated against real OpenAI error logs). Paste your full prompt, including tool/function schemas. Everything runs in your browser.

Handles English mixed with Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi, Malayalam and Arabic. gpt-realtime charges these scripts far more than any offline library predicts — roughly their UTF-8 byte count — so the verdict gates on that higher number and never wrongly approves a prompt.
